MUSIC

Welcome to the Music section of Miss Jacqui's website! Here you will find a collection of original songs and spoken word pieces that explore themes of identity, resilience, and social change. Drawing on her own experiences as a black woman with a disability, Miss Jacqui uses music as a powerful tool to challenge stereotypes and break down barriers. Her debut EP, "Perception," is a testament to her bold artistic vision and her commitment to creating a more inclusive and equitable world. So sit back, relax, and let Miss Jacqui's music take you on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment.
