PRESS

Miss Jacqui's work as a poet, songwriter, and performer has been featured in various publications and media outlets. Her thought-provoking work has been praised for its ability to challenge societal perceptions and inspire audiences to see the world differently. Miss Jacqui has been featured in Gal-Dem, URevolution, and Wellcome Collection among other outlets. She has also performed at a variety of venues and events, including the 2012 Paralympic Team Welcoming Ceremonies, Southbank Centre, The Barbican, and Roundhouse. Miss Jacqui's unique perspective as a black woman with a disability has made her a powerful voice in the arts, and she continues to use her platform to advocate for positive change and inspire others to be their authentic selves.
