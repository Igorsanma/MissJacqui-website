Miss Jacqui

Poet, Songwriter, and Spoken Word Artist.

Meet Miss Jacqui

Her mission is to challenge societal perceptions and inspire others to embrace their differences. As a wheelchair user, she knows firsthand what it means to work with the cards that you are dealt, and she wants her poetry and music to help her listeners see the world differently. With over 15 years of experience in the industry, Miss Jacqui has performed at some of the UK's most prestigious venues and worked with a variety of companies and organisations. Join her on her journey to create a more inclusive and equitable society through the power of art.
