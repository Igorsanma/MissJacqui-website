PERFORMANCE

Miss Jacqui is a performer at heart, and her live shows are not to be missed. With a passion for breaking down stereotypes, Miss Jacqui's performances are powerful, thought-provoking, and always inspiring.
"I want to break as many stereotypes as possible when it comes to me being a black woman with a disability,"
says Miss Jacqui, and her live shows are a testament to that goal. Whether she's performing spoken word poetry or singing her original songs, Miss Jacqui is always pushing the boundaries of what is possible.

With a background in theatre and a love for music that tells a story, Miss Jacqui's performances are a true reflection of her creative spirit. As a champion for multiple organizations, she is committed to using her art to create positive change and promote equality and community. So come and experience the magic of Miss Jacqui's live shows, and be prepared to see the world in a whole new way.
